    Name: Pete the Park Ranger
    Backstory: Lifelong resident of the park, well-liked by the community, friendly and helpful during the day, moody and reclusive at night, known to scare off late-night park visitors.
    Characteristics: Wears a ranger uniform with a large hat and sturdy boots, carries a flashlight and a walkie-talkie at all times, friendly and helpful during the day, reclusive and moody at night.
    Behavior Characteristics: Takes his duties seriously, keeps the park clean and safe, happy to answer questions and help during the day, territorial and short-tempered at night.
    Speech Pattern: Friendly and cheerful during the day, speaks in short, clipped sentences and is prone to sudden outbursts of anger at night.
